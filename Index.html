Tribute Page - Johan Cruyff

Johan Cruyff

The prophet of the goal

From birth to pros:

  • 1947 - Johan Cruyff is born the 25th of April in Betondorp, a suburb of Amsterdam.
  • 1957 - Exactly the day of his tenth birthday he is selected to be part of Ajax's youth squad.
  • 1961 - Cruyff wins his first league championship with the Ajax's youth squad.
  • 1963 - At the age of 16 years old Johan Cruyff signs his first professional contratct play for Ajax F.C.'s first team.

Carreer as a club football player:

  • 1964 - The professional debut is the 15th of November against Groningen, with the final resul of 1-3 for Ajax. His first arrives one week later against PSV, for an Ajax win of 5-0.
  • 1965 - Thanks to the poor results of Ajax th coach Vic Buckingham gets sacked, Rinus Michaels takes his place. It's the start of 'Total Voetball' era.
  • 1965/1969 - It's a golden era for Ajax: the team wins 3 consecutive Netherlands league championships and a league cup. Cruyff in a season averages 33 goals in 30 matches, but he and his team aren't able to defeat A.C. Milan in the Champions Cup of 1969.
  • 1970 - Johan starts wearing the iconic number 14 on his shirts.
  • 1971/1973 - Ajax is able to win his first Champions Cup defeting Panathinaikos with the score of 2-1, Cruyff serves an assist in this game and he's able to win his first 'Balon D'Or'. It's really a golden era for Cruyff and Ajax, the team his able to win 2 other Champions Cup (defeating Inter and Juve), an Intercontinental Cup and 6 National League titles from 1965 to 1973. Unfortunaly the relationship between Cruyff and Ajax is shaken when the decides to strip Johan from his captain status, leading him to move out from the club and sign a contract with Barcelona F.C.
  • 1974 - Barcelona wins his first La Liga title from 1960, the leader of the team is off course Johan Cruyff that closes the season with 16 goals, of wich one was a beautiful goal against Atletico de Madrid that got him the nickname of 'flying-dutchman'.
  • 1975 - This was a problematic season for both Barcelona and Cruyff, whom couldn't form a bond with coach Hennes Weisweiler. This and other factors led him to end his football carreer.
  • 1979/1981 - His manager and Father-in-law Cor Coster convinces him to play football in the U.S.A.'s N.A.S.L. League, first with the New York Cosmos, then with the L.A. Aztects and lastly with the Washington Diplomats. After 2 seasons it was time to return to Europe, and the first stage was Levante in Spain where he plaiyed just 10 games thanks to a fragile condition. After a brief parentesis with A.C. Milan, in 1981 Johan returned to Motherland Netherlands at Ajax.
  • 1982/1983 - With the return of Cruyff and the effort of 2 young stars such as Frank Rijkaard and Marco van Basten, Ajax is able to win 2 National Championships and a National Cup. Later in 1983 Johan decides to move out from Ajax to play for Feyenoord F.C..
  • 1984 - This season at Feyenoord sees Johan Cruyff playing in the insolite position of last defensive man, his leadership and skills along with the ones of a young Ruud Gullit help the team based in Rotterdam to win a National Championship (the nineth for the flying dutchman) and National Cup. After those wins it was time for 'The prophet of the goal' to end his football player carreer.

The journey with the National team:

  • 1965 - Johan makes his debut with the Netherlands youth squad in the European Championship
  • 1966 - It's time for him to debut with the Senior National team against Hungary, the match is a tie (2-2) but Johan is able to make is first goal.
  • 1971 - For the first Cruyff gets selected as the captain of the Oranges.
  • 1974 - The Netherlands footbal team coached by Rinus Michels amazes the world at the World Cup in Germany. The team plays a innovative way of football and off course Cruijff is the leader of the team. Unfortunaly the dream to bring the World Cup home is broken Germany during the final.
  • 1976 - The Netheralnds participate to the European Championship in Jugoslavia, but again can't win the trophy and instead they end up being in third place. It is said that one of the reason of the failure is due to the bad relationship between Johan Cruyff and Willem van Hanegem.
  • 1978 - Another World Cup takes place, this time in Argentina, but Johan Cruyff isn't part of the team. Officially it is because he is opposing the argentinian dictatorship but years later in an interview he confessed it was because he didn't have enough motivation to continue play with the natonal team.

Another challenge: being a coach

  • 1985 - Ajax grants the coach position to Johan Cruyff to replace Aad de Mos.
  • 1986/1988 - His time at Ajax is rich of successes, Cruyff wins 2 conscutive National Cups, and a Cup of Cups (14 years from the last Ajax's european trophy). The 4th of January 1988 he decides to leave the Ajax job for a new adventure at beloved Barcelona F.C.
  • 1989/1996 - Johan Cruyff journey at Barcelona is quite epic. Since his arrival the club sees a revolution in terms of working methods and structural development. In this years players like Pep Guardiola, José Mari Bakero, Txiki Begiristain, Guillermo Amor, Jon Andoni Goikoetxea, Ronald Koeman, Michael Laudrup and Hristo Stoičkov, Balon D'Or 1994, are coached by the flying dutchman. With all this talent led by Cruyff, Barcelona is able to win 4 La Liga titles, a Copa del Rey, and more importantly the club's first Champions League in the final of Wembley in 1992 against Sampdoria. The team however can't reapeat themselves in the final of 1994 against Arrigo Sacchi's A.C. Milan, the result is a brutal 0-4 in favor of the milanese team. Unfortunaly , since 1990, a lot of health problems such as heart attacks begin to happen to Cruijff, and he is forced to leave the club th 18th of May 1996.
  • 2009 - After many years Johan Cruyff accepts to coach the Catalan Selection.
  • 2013 - The 2nd of January takes place his last game as a coach: a draw between Cataluna and Nigeria with the score of 1-1.

Office work, director carreer:

  • 2008 - Johan Cruyff is appointed by Ajax F.C. as technical director.
  • 2010 - Is Barcelona time again: the flying dutchman is now the onorary president of the club, but the role lasts few months thanks of the arrival of the new president Sandro Rosell.
  • 2011/2012 - Ajax gets him another job opportunity as a manager. Things do not go well especially his relationship with the new managers Louis van Gaal, Martin Sturkenboom e Danny Blind is so deleterious that they take their board management fights to court. The judge rules in favor of Cruyff, but eventually the 4th of February 2012 he leaves the position. After all this mess at Ajax the flying dutchman decides to go to Mexico at Guadalajara as general director. The mexican adventure lasts not the long and Johan Cruyff leaves the club the 2nd of December of the same year.
  • 2015 - The last football job for Cruyff is again at Ajax F.C. as consulant, but the experience here is brief becouse of strong divergencies with the club.

The battle against cancer:

  • 2015 - The 15th of October Cruyff reveals to the world is battle against a lung cancer.
  • 2016 - Johan Cruyff dies the 24th of March in Barcelona at the age of 68 years old. The whole football world mourns is death and will always remembar is revolutionary contribute to the Game.
